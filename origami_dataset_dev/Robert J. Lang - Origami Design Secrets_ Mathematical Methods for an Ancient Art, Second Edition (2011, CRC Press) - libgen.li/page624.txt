             3. Fold four times, dividing the paper into eighths. 4. Fold eight times, dividing the paper into

             5. Fold 16 times, dividing the paper into 32nds. 6. Make a bunch of diagonal creases.
             Make each fold by bringing the mountain fold
             just below it to the one just above it (or vice-
