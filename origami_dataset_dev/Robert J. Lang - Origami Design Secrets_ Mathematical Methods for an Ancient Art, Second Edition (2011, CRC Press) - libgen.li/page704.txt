             Cicada Nymph


             Mark the edges of a 19×19 square grid; then use the
             edge markings to locate interior vertices. Note that
             here the “axial” contours are not located on the center
             line; this gives the base one extra unit of width. The
             extra creases near the top should be precreased before
             the collapse.
