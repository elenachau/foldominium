             Scarab Beetle


             Mark the edges of a 22×22 square grid. Use the edge
             markings to locate interior vertices. You can estimate
             the position of the axial+1 creases that run along the
             hypotenuse of the extended Pythagorean stretch from
             where they hit the edge relative to the edge markings.
             The creases in the middle are used in the shaping of
             the body.
