             Flying Walking Stick


             Mark the edges of a 74×74 square grid. Use the edge
             markings to locate the interior vertices. All axis-
             parallel creases are either axial or axial+1, but the
             middle points (that form the wing covers) make the
             collapse a bit challenging.
