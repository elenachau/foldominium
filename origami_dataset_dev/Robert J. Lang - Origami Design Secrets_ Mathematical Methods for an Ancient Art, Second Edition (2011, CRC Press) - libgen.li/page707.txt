             Scorpion HP


             Divide the side edges into 58 equal divisions. Then,
             starting from the middle, measure off 24 divisions in
             each direction, each horizontal division 1.155× larger
             than the vertical divisions. There will be a little paper
             left over at each edge; this gets folded inside. Use the
             divisions as the basis of a 30° grid for the creases.
